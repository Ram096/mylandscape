Learn how universal design enhances user experiences

Universal design is an approach where products and environments are created to be accessible and usable by everyone, without the necessity for specific adaptations or specialized designs, to the maximum extent feasible.

Accessibility is about more than compliance with standards. It’s about developing solutions to meet the needs of all users, with and without disabilities.

Quick Tip: Color Contrast Matters

    Color contrast significantly contributes to visibility, particularly for individuals with varying degrees of visual impairment or color blindness, fostering an inclusive online environment. High color contrast not only enhances the clarity of interactive elements but also mitigates eye strain, creating a more comfortable browsing experience for all users.
